Our Story

Where Nature Meets
Serenity

Poovar is a beautiful place in the south of Kerala where the Neyyar River meets the Arabian Sea. It is full of peaceful backwaters, green mangrove trees, and golden beaches.

Dreamvalley Backwater gives you a real boating experience through this natural area. You can enjoy the ride, see mangrove islands, watch birds, and enjoy beautiful sunsets from the boat.

Whether you come as a couple or with your family, our experienced boatmen will guide you safely and make your trip enjoyable and memorable.

Discover

Places You'll See

🌿 Mangrove Forest

Enjoy a calm boat ride through green mangrove trees and natural tunnels filled with birds and nature sounds.

🦜 Bird Observation

See beautiful birds like kingfishers, herons, and other birds in their natural place.

🥥 Coconut Island

A small island full of coconut trees, giving a peaceful and natural feeling.

🏖 Golden Sand Beach

Walk on clean golden sand where the river meets the sea.

🏠 Floating Cottages

See houses floating on water, giving a unique and beautiful view.

🍛 Floating Restaurant

Enjoy tasty Kerala food in a restaurant floating on the water.

🌄 Sunset

Watch a beautiful sunset where the river, backwater, and sea meet.

⛪ Mother Mary Statue

A peaceful statue of Mother Mary near the river, giving a calm and spiritual feeling.

🪨 Elephant Rock

A big rock that looks like an elephant, standing near the water.

🛶 AVM Canal

Travel through a narrow water path surrounded by green trees and nature.

🐎 Camel & Horse Riding

Enjoy fun rides on camel or horse near the riverside..

🏝 Poovar Island

A beautiful island that you can reach only by boat, full of nature and resorts.
